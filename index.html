Connecsion-Cloud based Digital Signage Solution

Connecsion, offers a complete end to end solution, from hardware-software to content management service, that allows you to educate,entertain and upsell your most important audience-the people who are your business.

Getting started in just 3 easy steps!

Step 1

Connect Connecsion Device to your TV via HDMI port.

Step 2

Login to your Connecsion account, choose template, add content and link it to your TV screen.

Step 3

Create a schedule for your screens to run different content based on time of the day and day of the week.

Connecsion-Online Application Portal

Connecsion's digital signage application also enables you to schedule content for different hours or days for your screens effortlessly from the web, using your computer or smartphone.

With this comprehensive application portal, you can upload images, audios and videos in our cloud and use it as content for your signage.
